The Round Table

Welcome to the course website for "King Arthur: Reality and Romance"

King Arthur is the most popular and most frequently revived Western hero from the Middle Ages to the current moment. This course examines the Arthurian story - Camelot, the knights of the Round Table, chivalry, and the Holy Grail - from its roots in the Middle Ages to its flourishing today.
